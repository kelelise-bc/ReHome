Our Mission

We believe every item deserves a second life. ReHome exists to connect people who no longer need something with people who do. Whether you’re clearing space or looking for essentials, we make reuse simple, local, and friendly. Our goal is less waste, more sharing, and a community that helps one another in small, meaningful ways.

What We Notice Every Day

Perfectly good items get tossed out because there isn’t an easy way to pass them along. At the same time, many families are searching for affordable things they need—furniture, tools, kids’ items, household basics.

ReHome closes that gap: post what you don’t need, discover what others are offering, and share instead of buying new. It’s convenient, community-driven, and better for everyone.

Our Values

  • Less Waste – Giving items new life instead of throwing them away.
  • Community – Neighbors helping neighbors.
  • Ease – Simple to use; anyone can participate.
  • Kindness – A small act can make a big difference.
