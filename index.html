A Place Where Every Item Finds a New Home

Welcome to ReHome

Post items you no longer need, browse posts from neighbors, or set alerts when something you're looking for becomes available. ReHome is built for sharing. It's fast, clutter-free and focused on giving useful things a second life in your community.

Browse Listings

See what’s available in your area — furniture, décor, tools, craft supplies, and more.

Listings are updated every day. If you see something you love, message the poster, arrange pickup, and give it a new home.

It’s first-come, first-served, so check back often.

Create a Post

Share something you want to pass along. Snap a photo, add a short description, and post it. That’s it.

Whether it’s a lamp, a chair, or a stack of books — someone else will appreciate it. Clear space. Help a neighbor. Feel good doing it.

Get Alerts

Never miss something you’re hoping for. Choose the categories you care about — home goods, office supplies, kids’ items, etc.

When a matching post goes live, you’ll get a quick notification. Simple, timely, and easy to cancel anytime.
